The song "Naalo Nenu" from the movie couple Friendly is a romantic tune that captures the essence of love and longing. It features the vocals of Sanjith Hegde and was composed by Aditya Ravindran. The lyrics, penned by Ramajogayya Sastry, express the desire to hold onto a moment of love and the wish to make it last forever. The song beautifully portrays the tender moments between the lead actors, Santosh Sobhan and Manasa Varanasi, and is a perfect addition to the film's musical theme.
