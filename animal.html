The song "Ammayi" from the Telugu movie "Animal"is a heartfelt ballad that expresses deep affection and admiration for a woman. Sung by Raghav Chaitanya and Pritam, the lyrics, penned by Anantha Sriram, beautifully capture the essence of love and longing. The song's music, composed by Jam8, blends traditional elements with contemporary beats, creating a captivating auditory experience. The lyrics highlight the enchanting qualities of the beloved, comparing her beauty to heavenly imagery, and emphasize the joy and satisfaction found in their relationship.
