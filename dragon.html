The song "Rise of Dragon" from the movie "Return of the Dragon"is an energetic dance number featuring Pradeep Ranganathan and Gautham Vasudev Menon. It was composed by Leon James and sung by him along with Nadisha Thomas. The song's upbeat beats and vibrant rhythm make it a hit, encouraging everyone to dance along. The film, directed by Ashwath Marimuthu, is produced by AGS Entertainment and stars a talented cast including KS Ravi Kumar, Mysskin, and Anupama Parameshwaran. The song has been well-received, adding to the excitement for the movie.
